**Last Updated: December 18, 2024**

## English Version

### Introduction

WordVault ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our vocabulary learning service.

### Information We Collect

#### Information You Provide

- **Favorite Words**: Words you mark as favorites are stored locally on your device
- **Learning History**: Your word viewing history is stored locally on your device
- **Quiz Progress**: Your quiz scores are stored locally on your device

**Important**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. All your data remains on your device.

#### Information Collected by Third Parties

**Google AdMob**  
Our App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

- Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
- IP address
- Device type and operating system
- App usage data
- Location data (if permitted)

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### How We Use Information

#### Local Data

- To provide personalized word recommendations
- To track your learning progress
- To save your favorite words
- To maintain your learning history

#### Advertising

- To display relevant advertisements
- To measure ad performance
- To provide free access to the App

### Notifications and Alarms

**Daily Word Notifications**  
Our App uses the following permissions to deliver daily vocabulary notifications:

- **Scheduled Exact Alarms** (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM): We schedule notifications at your preferred time each day to help you maintain a consistent learning routine. This permission allows us to deliver timely reminders for your vocabulary learning.
- **Boot Completion**: We reschedule your daily notifications when your device restarts to ensure you don't miss your learning reminders
- **Notification Display**: We display notifications to remind you to learn new words

**Important**: 
- All notification preferences are stored locally on your device
- You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings or within the App
- We do not collect or transmit any data about your notification interactions

### Data Storage and Security

- All user data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences
- We do not maintain servers or databases containing user information
- Your data is not backed up to cloud services by our App
- If you uninstall the App, all local data will be deleted

### Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

**Google AdMob**
- Purpose: Display advertisements
- Data collected: Device identifiers, usage data
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Google Play Services**
- Purpose: App distribution and updates
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Advertisements

#### Ad Types

- **Banner Ads**: Displayed on the home screen
- **Interstitial Ads**: Shown when switching categories
- **Rewarded Ads**: Optional ads for bonus quiz content

#### Ad Personalization

You can opt out of personalized advertising by:

1. Going to your device Settings
2. Selecting Google > Ads
3. Enabling "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

### Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is designed for general audiences and does not contain age-inappropriate content.

### Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your data (stored locally on your device)
- Delete your data (by uninstalling the App or clearing app data)
- Opt out of personalized ads (through device settings)

### Data Retention

- Local data is retained until you delete it or uninstall the App
- Ad-related data is retained according to Google's privacy policy

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

### Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

**Email**: [azddin.anaddam@gmail.com]
